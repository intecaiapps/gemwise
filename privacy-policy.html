GemWise — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11.07.2026

1.Who we are

GemWise is an iOS app that identifies minerals and gemstones from photos you take. The app is provided by INTEC SFERA sp. z o.o., ul. Św. Filipa 23/3, 31-150 Kraków, Poland (“we”, “us”). We are the data controller for the processing described here.

Questions or requests: magistratorius@gmail.com.

2.What we do not do

  • No user accounts, no registration, no email or name collection.
  • No advertising and no ad tracking. We do not track you across other companies’ apps or websites, so no App Tracking Transparency prompt is needed.
  • We never sell or share your data for money or for cross-context behavioural advertising.
  • We do not store your photos on our servers. We have no server that keeps them.

Please do not include people, documents or other sensitive content in the photos you submit. The app only needs the stone.

4.How photos are handled

When you request an identification, the photo is sent to the Google Gemini API for processing and the result is returned to your device. The photo is transmitted for processing only. We do not keep a copy on any server of ours, and we do not use your photos to train AI models. Google processes the image under its own API terms and data policies; see [PLACEHOLDER: link to Google Gemini API terms / privacy policy].

The original photo stays in your device’s photo library or camera roll as normal — that copy is under your control, not ours.

8.Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, you have the right to access your data, correct it, delete it, restrict or object to processing, and to data portability. Where processing is based on our legitimate interest, you may object at any time.

Because the app has no accounts and we hold no identifiers that point to you, we usually cannot link any data to a specific person. If you contact us with a request, we may not be able to identify you in our systems (GDPR Art. 11) — in that case we will tell you so and explain what you can do yourself, such as deleting your local history or contacting Apple about purchase data.

To exercise a right, email magistratorius@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority — in Poland, the President of the Personal Data Protection Office (Prezes Urzędu Ochrony Danych Osobowych, UODO), ul. Stawki 2, 00-193 Warszawa.

9.California residents (CCPA/CPRA)

In the past 12 months we have not sold personal information and have not shared it for cross-context behavioural advertising. We do not use or disclose sensitive personal information for purposes that require a right to limit. The categories we handle are described in section 3; the categories of recipients are listed in section 5.

California residents have the right to know, delete and correct personal information, and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising those rights. Send requests to magistratorius@gmail.com. The same practical limit applies: without an account, we generally cannot connect data to you, and we will say so rather than guess.

10.Children

GemWise is rated 4+ but is a general-audience app. It is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, contact us and we will look into it.

11.Security

Data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. No system is perfectly secure, but keeping photos off our infrastructure and avoiding accounts means there is very little about you for anyone to take.
